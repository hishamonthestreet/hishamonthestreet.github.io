About

I’m Hisham, a street photographer from KL, Malaysia who is drawn to quiet frames, reflections, and the empty spaces between people. Most of my work is made while walking with no fixed plan, letting light and timing decide the pictures.

This site is a small selection of images that feel honest to how the streets look and feel to me.
