About

I’m Hisham Ahmad, a street photographer from Kuala Lumpur who gravitates toward layered scenes, deep shadows, reflections, and the quiet spaces between people. Most of my photographs are made while walking with no clear route, allowing light, timing, and fragments of the city to arrange themselves in the frame.

This site is a small edit of work that feels honest to how the streets look and feel to me.
