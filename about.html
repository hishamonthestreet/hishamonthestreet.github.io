About

I’m Hisham Ahmad, a street photographer from KL, Malaysia who is drawn to reflections, blocked views, and the empty spaces between people. Most of these pictures are made while walking without a fixed plan, letting light and timing decide what stays.

This site is a small edit of work that feels honest to how the streets look and feel to me.
